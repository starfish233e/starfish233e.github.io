一道有趣的奥赛题

问题

设xn=∑k=1n1k−2n，证明lim⁡n→∞xn存在，并求出该极限设x_n = \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{1}{\sqrt{k}} - 2\sqrt{n}，证明\lim_{n \to \infty}x_{n}存在，并求出该极限 设xn​=k=1∑n​k​1​−2n​，证明n→∞lim​xn​存在，并求出该极限

解答

解：
[
\begin{aligned}
x_{n} &= \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{2}{2\sqrt{k}} - 2\sqrt{n} \
&\geqslant \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{2}{\sqrt{k} + \sqrt{k+1}} - 2\sqrt{n} \
&= 2\sum_{k=1}^{n} (\sqrt{k+1} - \sqrt{k}) - 2\sqrt{n} \
&= 2\sqrt{n+1} - 2\sqrt{n} - 2 \
&\geqslant -2
\end{aligned}
]

因此数列 {xn}\{x_n\}{xn​} 有下界 (-2)，接下来证明 {xn}\{x_n\}{xn​} 是单调递减函数：

[
\begin{aligned}
x_{n+1} - x_{n} &= \frac{1}{\sqrt{n+1}} + 2\sqrt{n} - 2\sqrt{n+1} \
&= \frac{1}{\sqrt{n+1}} - \frac{1}{\frac{\sqrt{n} + \sqrt{n+1}}{2}} < 0
\end{aligned}
]

根据单调有界函数必有极限，可以证明数列 {xn}\{x_n\}{xn​} 极限存在。接下来求解该极限。[1]

x2n=∑k=12n1k−22n,2xn=2∑k=1n12k−22n,x2n−2xn=∑k=12n1k−2∑k=1n12k=∑k=12n(−1)n−1n\begin{aligned} x_{2n} &= \sum_{k=1}^{2n} \frac{1}{\sqrt{k}} - 2\sqrt{2n}, \\ \sqrt{2}x_{n} &= 2 \sum_{k=1}^{n}\frac{1}{\sqrt{2k}} - 2\sqrt{2n}, \\ x_{2n} - \sqrt{2}x_{n} &= \sum_{k=1}^{2n}\frac{1}{\sqrt{k}} - 2 \sum_{k=1}^{n}\frac{1}{\sqrt{2k}} = \sum_{k=1}^{2n} \frac{(-1)^{n-1}}{\sqrt{n}} \end{aligned} x2n​2​xn​x2n​−2​xn​​=k=1∑2n​k​1​−22n​,=2k=1∑n​2k​1​−22n​,=k=1∑2n​k​1​−2k=1∑n​2k​1​=k=1∑2n​n​(−1)n−1​​

由交错级数的敛散性可知右边级数收敛，两边取极限((n \to \infty ))

设lim⁡n→∞xn=λ,lim⁡n→∞x2n−lim⁡n→∞2xn=lim⁡n→∞∑k=12n(−1)n−1k,λ=11−2lim⁡n→∞∑k=12n(−1)n−1k=ζ(12).设 \lim_{n \to \infty}x_{n} = \lambda, \\ \lim_{n \to \infty}x_{2n} - \lim_{n \to \infty}\sqrt{2}x_{n} = \lim_{n \to \infty}\sum_{k=1}^{2n}\frac{(-1)^{n-1}}{\sqrt{k}}, \\ \lambda = \frac{1}{1 - \sqrt{2}}\lim_{n \to \infty}\sum_{k=1}^{2n}\frac{(-1)^{n-1}}{\sqrt{k}} = \zeta\left({\frac{1}{2}}\right). 设n→∞lim​xn​=λ,n→∞lim​x2n​−n→∞lim​2​xn​=n→∞lim​k=1∑2n​k​(−1)n−1​,λ=1−2​1​n→∞lim​k=1∑2n​k​(−1)n−1​=ζ(21​).
